Children Ministry

  • Proverbs 22:6 Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it.
  • Isaiah 54:13 All your children shall be taught by the LORD, and great shall be the peace of your children.
  • Children are the future leaders of God, so the purpose of our ministry is to help them to understand & obey, encourage them to set their hope on Christ and to enable them into a lifelong & ever-growing relationship with our Lord Jesus Christ. Our main focus is to help them to walk in the way of the Lord.
  • Children are taught the Bible lessons and the art of Worship. They are encouraged and trained to memorize the scriptures and to participate in bible quiz & contests like JBQ. Sunday classes are held simultaneously during the church services.
